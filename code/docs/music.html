MP3 Music Setup

Overview
Before you can add files to the system, they must be uploaded to your web server via FTP. FTP is better and faster than standard web form uploads. A lot of excellent, free FTP software exists and the following are recommended:

FileZilla
WinSCP
Cyberduck
In Maian Music I have tried to make adding files as easy as possible.
Step 1 Upload Full MP3 Files
Upload your full MP3 downloads into your secure folder, in any structure. Sub folders are supported.
Step 2 Upload Preview MP3 Files
Upload your preview MP3 files into the 'previews' folder, in any structure. Sub folders are supported. These should be short snippets of your main tracks. Maybe 20/30 second previews. There are free utilities that can help you create short snippets, here are a few:

Audacity (Recommended)
MP3 Direct Cut (Recommended)
MP3 Cutter
MP3 Cutter & Editor
Eusing Free MP3 Cutter
Audacity & MP3 Direct Cut are portable. MP3 Direct Cut offers batch processing.
Step 3 Upload MP3 Cover Art
Upload your MP3 cover art into the 'cover-art' folder, in any structure. Sub folders are supported.
Step 4 Add Music to System
Log into your Maian Music admin control panel to add your music. Add collections first, then tracks.
