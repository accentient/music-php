Moss Export (Mini One Stop Shop)

Overview
If you live in the EU and are selling digital goods, you MUST register for MOSS, whether you are currently VAT registered or not. At the end of each tax quarter (or month), you are required to declare tax on digital goods sold to EU customers. The export screen makes it easier for you to generate an overview of MOSS sales.

Note that the CSV export also includes the country codes as this may be required when you submit your report.
