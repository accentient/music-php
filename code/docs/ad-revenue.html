Revenue Overview

Overview
At a glance view of revenue for a given year with options to filter by year, tax type and country. Useful for tax returns. Result view can be exported to CSV.
Search
To search and change the revenue display click the button. This opens the search panel.
Search - Enter Year (4 Digits)
Enter 4 digit year, ie: 2015, 2016 etc. Defaults to current year.
Search - Tax Country
Filter by tax country
Search - Country Tax Preference
If filtering by tax country, this option lets you drill down to a specific tax revenue for that country. 'Tangible' would show ONLY sales revenue where the tangible tax country was the filter country and 'digital' would show the same for digital.
Export View to CSV
Click button to export current view to CSV
