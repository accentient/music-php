Fetch MNS - Setup
1: Sign up for a Fetch account.
2: Log into your Fetch merchant account. Click "Channel Settings > Web Payments and Invoice Payments > Integration Settings" to set up your shared / secret key.
3: Enter applicable details in your Maian Music admin area:

Payment Gateways > Fetch

Note that your account ID is NOT your merchant ID/number. Click "Profile > View Accounts" for the correct account ID.
Fetch - Callback Url
The callback url for all operations is:

http://www.example.com/callback/fetch.php
https://www.example.com/callback/fetch.php (SSL)

Where 'example.com' is your own domain.
