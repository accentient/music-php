Paypoint - Setup
1: Sign up for a Paypoint account to receive your merchant id and password.
2: Log into your Paypoint account to create your remote password.
3: Enter applicable details in your Maian Music admin area:

Payment Gateways > Paypoint
Paypoint - Callback Url
The callback url for all operations is:

http://www.example.com/callback/paypoint.php
https://www.example.com/callback/paypoint.php (SSL)

Where 'example.com' is your own domain.
